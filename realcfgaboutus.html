Why we chose to do this...

We decided as a group to focus on the topic of mental health, which has been on the rise in recent years, particularly with the increasing number of young people being diagnosed with mental health issues. It can be difficult to express how you feel on a daily basis, and this difficulty is frequently linked to mental health, particularly when you can't put your feelings into words or think clearly about them.

We decided to make a website based on the TikTok-famous reversible octopus plushie, which has been shown to help people physically express their emotions by allowing them to flip its two colourful sides (one happy side, one sad side). Our website allows users to choose an emotion they are experiencing right now and receive a positive quote that will hopefully make them smile.

So tell us, how are you feeling today?
